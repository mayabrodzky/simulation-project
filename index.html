Our story

Polaris was built as a compact, teachable product that helps teams reason about decisions with quick simulations and crisp visuals. The goal is to reduce back-and-forth, and let anyone — students, managers, or partners — explore scenarios in minutes, not weeks.

FAQ

Do I need to install anything?

No. Polaris runs on the web and works on modern browsers.

Can we adapt it for different domains?

Yes. The simulations are generic and can be tailored to many industries.

How is my data stored?

For the demo we use Supabase (Postgres) with strict RLS. Production setups can use your own cloud.
